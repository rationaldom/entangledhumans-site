Why **entangledHumans**? Because **Entanglement** arises in disparate areas of my life. Shibari or Kinbaku, literally entangles humans. Shibari is something I study and Kinbaku is a performance art that I appreciate.  Entangled is also a state that two or more social beings can find themselves in. Life is rich in complexity, uncertainty, and messiness.  

Finally, as a card-carrying PhD theoretical physicist,  entangled quantum states provoke endless fascination. Entangled states say something deep about the nature of physical reality. What exactly they tell us remains poorly understood. Finally, it's just artistic license and word-play folks, there's no reason to believe in deep connection between entanglement in the quantum sense and the other meanings of entanglement.
